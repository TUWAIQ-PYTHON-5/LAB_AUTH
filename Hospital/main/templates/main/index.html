Cardiology

At Dooha Hospital, our world-class team of cardiologists diagnoses and treats a full range of heart conditions in adults, in line with the patients' requirements. Our professionally trained medical team uses cutting-edge medical innovations to screen, evaluate and treat cardiovascular disorders.

DEPARTMENT OF NEUROLOGY

Our medical team of neurologists at the Dooha Hospital is fully prepared to examine, diagnose and treat disorders of the nervous system. This includes the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves and muscles, and much more.

THERAPEUTIC NUTRITION

Dooha Hospitals believe in the importance of clinical nutrition and its role in treating many diseases, in addition to helping to improve the quality of health and life of the patient, so we are keen to provide one of the most efficient clinical nutrition clinics in the Middle East.

EYE SURGERIES

We believe in satisfying the patient in a distinguished manner through the methods of treatment, prevention and dealing with eye-related diseases that we offer at the Dooha Hospital. Our team of ophthalmologists skillfully combines leading technology and extensive experience to provide you with safe and effective ophthalmological care.
